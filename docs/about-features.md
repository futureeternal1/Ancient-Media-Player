# Ancient Media Player

Ancient Media Player is an all-in-one media app for music, radio, TV, YouTube, and local video. It brings offline playback and online streaming together in one place, while also giving users control over how their content is organized and how the home screen is displayed.

## What The App Does

Ancient Media Player lets users:

- Play local music stored on their device.
- Browse songs by songs, albums, artists, folders, playlists, genres, history, last added, and most played.
- Shuffle music and start playback from different library views.
- Choose folders and play audio directly from those folders.
- Save and restore playback queues.
- Add and play internet radio stations.
- Create, edit, and delete radio stations.
- Group radio stations into categories.
- Add, edit, and delete radio categories.
- Add thumbnails to radio stations and radio categories.
- Add images by link or by uploading from the device.
- Crop, resize, and compress uploaded images before saving.
- Watch TV and Christian free movie channels in a dedicated TV section.
- Add, edit, and delete TV channels.
- Group TV channels into categories.
- Add, edit, and delete TV categories.
- Add thumbnails to TV channels and TV categories.
- Add and manage YouTube channels inside the app.
- Edit and delete saved YouTube channels.
- Open saved YouTube channels for viewing.
- Browse and play local videos stored on the device.
- View videos in a dedicated video section similar to the music library experience.
- Use a customizable home screen with shortcut tiles for key features.
- Manage which shortcuts and features appear on the home screen.
- Access extra app sections through the bottom navigation and `More` area.

## Main Sections

### Music

Users can play audio files from their device and browse them through a full media library interface.

### Radio

Users can add their own radio stations, organize them into categories, and play online radio streams.

### TV

Users can watch saved TV and Christian free movie channels, organize them by category, and manage entries with custom thumbnails.

### YouTube

Users can save YouTube channels they want quick access to and open them from inside the app flow.

### Videos

Users can browse and play video files stored on the device in a dedicated video section.

### Home Screen Management

Users can decide which shortcuts, icons, and features are shown on the home page so the app can match their needs.

## What Users Can Do With Ancient Media Player

- Use it as an offline music player.
- Use it as an internet radio player.
- Use it as a Christian TV and free movie hub.
- Use it as a quick-launch hub for YouTube channels they follow.
- Use it as a local video player for personal video files.
- Organize radio and TV content into categories.
- Add artwork and thumbnails to make sections more visual.
- Personalize the home screen for fast access to favorite features.
