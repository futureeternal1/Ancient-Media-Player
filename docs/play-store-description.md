# Ancient Media Player Play Store Copy

## Short Description

Music, radio, TV, YouTube, and local video in one customizable media app.

## Full Description

Ancient Media Player is a complete media app designed for users who want more than just a music player. It brings together offline music playback, internet radio, TV streaming, YouTube channel access, and local video playback in one organized experience.

With Ancient Media Player, you can listen, watch, organize, and personalize your media from one app.

### Key Features

- Play music stored on your device.
- Browse by songs, albums, artists, folders, playlists, genres, history, last added, and most played.
- Shuffle songs and play from multiple library views.
- Choose audio folders directly from storage.
- Add and play custom radio stations.
- Create, edit, and delete radio stations.
- Organize radio stations into categories.
- Add, edit, and delete radio categories.
- Watch TV and Christian free movie channels.
- Add, edit, and delete TV channels.
- Group TV channels into categories.
- Add, edit, and delete TV categories.
- Add YouTube channels for quick access.
- Edit and delete saved YouTube channels.
- Browse and play videos stored on your device.
- Add thumbnails for stations, channels, and categories.
- Upload images from your device or use image links.
- Crop, resize, and compress uploaded images before saving.
- Customize your app home screen by choosing which shortcuts and features are shown.
- Use the `More` section in bottom navigation for extra app areas.

### Built For Flexible Media Use

Ancient Media Player can be used as:

- A local music player
- An internet radio player
- A Christian TV and free movie channel app
- A local video player
- A YouTube channel launcher and viewing hub

### Personal And Customizable

You are not limited to only built-in content. You can add your own radio stations, TV channels, YouTube channels, categories, and artwork, then arrange your home screen around the features you use most.
